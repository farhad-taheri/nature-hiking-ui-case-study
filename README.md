## 🧠 Problem
Many people who love hiking struggle to find reliable trails, safety information, and easy trip planning tools in one place.

## 🎯 Goal
Design a simple, clean, and user-friendly mobile app that helps users discover hiking trails, plan trips, and explore nature confidently.

---

## 👤 Target Users
- Beginner hikers
- Nature lovers
- Travelers looking for outdoor experiences

---

## 🔍 Research Insights
- Users want **easy navigation and simple UI**
- Safety info (weather, difficulty) is very important
- Visual content (images of trails) increases trust

---

## 🧭 User Flow
1. Open app  
2. Browse hiking trails  
3. View trail details  
4. Save or start trip
